## 3. LinkedIn-style post

## 4. Claude / Anthropic community post

> **Carlos Council — a multi-agent strategy "council" you can self-host**
>
> I wanted to see what happens when you stop asking one model for one answer and instead let
> several specialized agents disagree. Carlos Council runs a business brief through four
> phases — parallel analysis by five specialists, synthesis + clarification questions, two
> rounds of moderated debate, and a final 10-point plan — all streamed live over SSE.
>
> Stack: FastAPI backend (OpenAI SDK against a configurable base URL, so it works with
> Claude via a compatible gateway, OpenRouter, or local models), Next.js 16 frontend, SQLite.
> The key stays server-side; the browser only talks to a proxy.
>
> It's MIT, early, and I'd love feedback on the orchestration design and prompts. Repo:
> [link]
