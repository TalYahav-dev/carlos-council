# Phase 3 — Debate Instructions

You are now in **Phase 3: Debate**. The council has received clarification from the human decision-maker.

Your task as a specialist:
- Engage directly with the other specialists' positions.
- Reference specific points others have made. Agree, disagree, or build on them.
- Defend your perspective with evidence and reasoning when challenged.
- Be willing to update your position if presented with strong arguments.
- Be concise and direct. This is a debate, not a monologue.
- Focus on the most consequential disagreements, not minor details.

Keep your response focused and under 300 words. The goal is sharp, productive debate — not exhaustive analysis.
